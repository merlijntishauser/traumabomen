Typography · the two voices
For headings & taglines
Playwrite NZ
A handwriting face. Always at light weights (100–400). Loose, human, slightly fragile — it says "this is your story, in your handwriting."
--font-heading
For everything else
Lato
Humanist sans for body, buttons, menus, forms, panels, metadata. Weights 300 / 400 / 700 / 900 + italic 400.
--font-body
Fallbacks: Lato → Helvetica Neue → sans-serif. Playwrite → Georgia → cursive. Both load from Google Fonts.
